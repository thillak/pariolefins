About Us

Pari Olefins manufactures and distributes high quality film, fiber, and webbing for industrial, commercial, and consumer uses. Our proprietary blend of resins and engineered master batch offers superior performance for a multitude of uses, from daily use to uniquely demanding speciality applications. Pari Olefins' inception occurred with the acquisition of Winzen Film & Fiber, Inc. on December 4th, 2012. Founded in 1949, Winzen Film & Fiber produced speciality films for high altitude research balloons used by the U.S. military and NASA. We hope to continue Winzen's legacy by improving the world and providing value to our customers and suppliers.
